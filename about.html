About Us

As a husband-and-wife team, we work together on every detail, inside and out. A home for your family, built by our family. That’s what you’ll find at Virani Custom Homes.

Annell Bailey

Annell left the tropical breezes of the Dominican Republic to study Business at Bob Jones University in Greenville, S.C. She started her career in banking, until she met John, got married and ended up in the homebuilding business.

If a Virani home seems different from everything else you’ve seen on the market, that’s because Annell brings a little bit of Caribbean warmth to every design project. Can’t decide on just the right color or finish? Ask Annell! She’ll be happy to share her ideas.

John Bailey

John has been an entrepreneur and business owner since he was 14 years old. Honesty, dedication and hard work are the values that he lives by. You’ll find him on the jobsite all hours of the day, overseeing every detail to make sure the work is done right and you’re 100% satisfied.

John is a Licensed General Contractor, with additional licenses in electrical, plumbing and HVAC.
